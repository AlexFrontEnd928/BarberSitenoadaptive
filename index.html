BARBERS

I’ve been in the hairdressing industry since I was 16, starting in a small barber shop at the top of my Mum’s road. I have been lucky enough to travel to many different countries in my life so far, and even luckier to be able to bring my job with me. I have cut hair in hostels, on beaches, in back gardens; you name it across South East Asia, Australia and New Zealand. Whatever language or destination the principle is exactly the same, to make someone look better, but more importantly, feel better about themselves.
